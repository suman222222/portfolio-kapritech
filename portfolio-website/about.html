About KapriTech

We are a small, passionate software studio founded by Suman Kapri. Our mission is to build beautiful, functional digital products that help people and businesses thrive.

Our Story

KapriTech started as a solo journey in 2025. Suman Kapri, a self-taught developer with a passion for clean code and design, began building projects to solve real-world problems. What started as a personal portfolio evolved into a full-fledged studio serving students, startups, and small businesses.

Today, we focus on delivering high-quality websites and web applications that are accessible, performant, and a joy to use.

Our Values

  • ✨ Quality First – We never compromise on code quality or user experience.
  • 🤝 Transparency – We communicate openly and honestly with our clients.
  • 📚 Continuous Learning – We stay up-to-date with the latest technologies and best practices.
